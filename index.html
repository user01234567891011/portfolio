The :user-valid and :user-invalid pseudo-classes

Working with forms is now more convenient thanks to the new :user-valid and :user-invalid pseudo-classes. Unlike :valid and :invalid, they activate only after user interaction, preventing unwanted effects on page load. However, most browsers don't support them yet, so styles may still apply immediately. To avoid this, use :focus, :focus-within, or add a JavaScript handler.
